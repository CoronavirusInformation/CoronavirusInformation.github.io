Coronavirus/Covid-19 is a virus that causes a disease
. When contracting the disease, you could either suffer
from mild, to medium, and then severe symptoms.
Mild cases can recover on their own, but medium
or severe may need to be hospitalized. Some of
the many symptoms of the disease are… Dry cough,
fever, runny nose, any type of ache, tiredness,
sore throat, and loss of taste and smell. Many
children can go symptomless, and spread Covid-19
without knowing, which is why we should take
this seriously. Ways to avoid the disease are: social distance,
staying home if you have any symptoms, and always wear a face mask,
only going outside when needed, and sanitizing/
washing hands often.

In Canada, the government has taken action on schools, which
include… parents having the choice of sending their child to
school or going online, if children were to go to school, they
would always need to wear face masks and sanitize often. For
highschool students, they have groups, such as A, and B. For
example, group A goes to school on one day, and group B goes
on the other for every week. The school times for highschool
students are also cut down by about an hour. Students taking
online classes will have to join Google Meets/Zoom regularly,
and do assignments online.
